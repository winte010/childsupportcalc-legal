About This Calculator

Child Support Calculator is a professional tool designed to help parents, attorneys, and mediators estimate child support obligations according to Alabama state guidelines. Our calculator implements the same formulas used by Alabama courts, including income schedules, self-support reserves, and adjustments for shared physical custody.
