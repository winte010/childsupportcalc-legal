Delete Your Account

Child Support Calculator by Juno Technology

We understand you may wish to delete your account and associated data from the Child Support Calculator app. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

⚠️ Important: Account Deletion is Permanent

Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from within the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Child Support Calculator app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (tap the gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the confirmation dialog carefully
  6. Confirm deletion by tapping "Delete My Account"
  7. Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Email Support

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at support@junotech.app with the following information:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Information: Your email address, name, and authentication credentials
  • Calculation Data: All child support calculations, income data, and saved scenarios
  • Usage History: Your app usage patterns and activity logs
  • Subscription Information: Your subscription status and history (note: active subscriptions must be cancelled separately through the App Store or Google Play Store)
  • Preferences: All app settings and customizations

Data Retention Period

Your account and personal data will be deleted within 30 days of your deletion request. Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics and legal compliance purposes, but it will not be associated with your identity.

What Happens to Your Subscription

Important: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately:

  • For iOS/Apple App Store: Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Child Support Calculator → Cancel Subscription
  • For Android/Google Play Store: Go to Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Child Support Calculator → Cancel Subscription

If you delete your account while you have an active subscription, you will lose access immediately but may still be charged until you cancel through the App Store or Google Play Store.

Data We May Retain

While we delete your personal information, we may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction Records: Payment records may be retained for tax and accounting purposes (up to 7 years as required by law)
  • Legal Compliance: Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data that cannot be linked back to you

Before You Delete

Before deleting your account, please consider:

  • Export Your Data: Use the "Export My Data" feature in the app's Settings to download a copy of your calculations
  • Cancel Subscription: Cancel your subscription first to avoid being charged after deletion
  • Outstanding Issues: Resolve any outstanding support tickets or billing issues
  • Temporary Deactivation: If you're unsure, you can simply stop using the app instead of deleting your account

Questions or Issues?

If you have questions about account deletion or encounter any issues, please contact our support team before proceeding with deletion.
